About

I am 25 and I love developing applications for Desk, Web and Mobile.

I have a nerdy knowledge about programming in C, C++ and JavaScript. I am a trained professional in Image Processing, CAD & 3D Modelling, Shell Scripting and Socket Programming. I have fair experience of configuring apps to work on Facebook, GitHub, OpenShift and Heroku Platforms. My latest endeavours are open source projects in JavaScript, NodeJS, AngularJS, ExpressJS, MongoDB, PHP, Android and HTML5. I love designing on Photoshop.

In the past I have worked largely on Windows Platforms, including developing solutions in MFC, COM, CLR and Visual C++.

I was born in the temple city, Bhubaneswar, IN and currently camping in Pune, IN. I did my B.Tech in CSE from Synergy Institute of Engineering and Technology. I stayed in Mumbai, IN for a while.

I am a hardcore Music addict and Open Source Enthusiast. Besides being hooked to my Notebook, I love going to rock gigs and night-outs with my buddies. I believe in God but not in Symbols. I trust myself. I love being me.
